About Us

Grand Mark Hospital was established in 2016 by Dr. Mariam Binte Mahfuz(C193223) (also along with C193203 & C193228), renowned as the architect of modern healthcare in Bangladesh. Grand Mark Hospital has emerged as South Asia's foremost integrated healthcare services provider and has a robust presence across the healthcare ecosystem, including Hospitals, Pharmacies, Primary Care & Diagnostic Clinics and several retail health models.
